"Because the Individual Matters"

With a friendly and relaxed teaching style, our primary goal is to help children enjoy learning and develop confidence in their abilities. All our lessons are tailored to individual needs, because for us it is the individual that matters.

As well as face-to-face tuition, we also offer tuition online using Zoom or other popular web conferencing tools such as MS Teams.

ABOUT US

As professional home tutors, we feel privileged to help children with their school work and watch them grow in confidence and ability. These are attributes that will help them not just at school, but also in everyday life and even in their future workplace.

Our team consists of qualified teachers with extensive experience in education. We specialize in tutoring children who range in age from 7 to 16 years old. With degrees in our respective subjects, we bring both expertise and passion to every lesson.

We pride ourselves on being able to establish an excellent rapport with all of our students, essential to helping them develop an enthusiasm for the subject. We plan and prepare lessons and work depending on the requirements of each child, aiming to find the appropriate level that will stretch them in the required area. Sometimes tasks are challenging and do require some effort but the results can be hugely rewarding.

ENGLISH

One-to-one English tuition, tailored to your child's needs

Qualified up to degree level in this subject, we help children of all ages from primary school through to those studying GCSE and A-Level English language and English literature. Typical areas of support and guidance include:

  • Early reading skills
  • Reading comprehension
  • Creative and analytical writing
  • Spelling, grammar and punctuation
  • Literature analysis
  • Exam preparation and coaching

For children who will soon be sitting their exams, we can help them with comprehensive exam preparation and strategic coaching techniques.

MATHS

One-to-one maths tuition, tailored to your child's needs

We are very familiar with the programmes of study for mathematics within the National Curriculum and we tutor Key Stages 1, 2, 3 and 4.

Using a variety of resources to make the experience more engaging, we provide tailored tuition on:

  • Numbers and calculations
  • Fractions, decimals, and percentages
  • Algebra and equations
  • Graphs and statistics
  • Geometry and shapes
  • Probability and data handling
  • GCSE and A-Level mathematics
